How does a QR code work?

A QR code (short for Quick Response code) is a special type of square-shaped barcode that stores information. It looks like a pattern of black and white squares. You can scan it using the camera on your smartphone or a special QR scanner.

QR codes make it easy to get information quickly without typing. Just point your phone camera at it, and it will do the rest for you.
