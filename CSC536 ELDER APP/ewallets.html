What is an E-Wallet?

An e-wallet, or digital wallet, is an app or online service that lets you store money, make payments, and track transactions using your phone or computer.

How to Use an E-Wallet:

  • Download a trusted e-wallet app (like PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay, or others).
  • Register your account with your email or phone number.
  • Add money to your wallet using your bank account or card.
  • Scan QR codes or tap your phone to pay at stores.
  • Use it to send or receive money from friends and family.

Benefits of Using an E-Wallet:

  • Convenient: Pay quickly without cash or cards.
  • Secure: Many use encryption, PINs, or face recognition.
  • Track Spending: See where your money goes with digital receipts.
  • Rewards: Some wallets offer discounts or points.
  • Contactless: Great for hygiene and fast payments.

With an e-wallet, your phone becomes your wallet. It's safe, smart, and simple!
